The Best Movies According to Joshua

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Shawshank Redemption

A timeless story of hope, friendship, and resilience that masterfully explores the human spirit in the face of injustice.

The Dark Knight

A gripping, morally complex superhero epic that redefined the genre through powerful performances and philosophical depth.

Top Gun Maverick

A rare sequel that surpasses its predecessor with stunning aerial cinematography, emotional storytelling, and pure cinematic exhilaration.
